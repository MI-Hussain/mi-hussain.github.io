### About

I specialize in **vision-language modeling**, **medical imaging AI**, and scalable training workflows. My current work involves fine-tuning and evaluating multimodal LLMs (e.g. MedGemma / PaliGemma variants) for visual question answering in radiology and broader scientific AI applications.

I enjoy translating research ideas into reproducible, performant systems while keeping an eye on interpretability and clinical usefulness.
